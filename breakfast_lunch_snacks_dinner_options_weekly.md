## **Weekly Fat Loss Plan** (Repeat for 4 Weeks)

### **Daily Schedule Overview**

* **Morning** → Workout (fasted or with lemon water)
* **Post-Workout** → Breakfast (high protein + moderate carbs)
* **Lunch** → Balanced plate (protein + fiber + complex carbs)
* **Evening Snack** → Light protein or fruit
* **Dinner** → Lightest meal of the day (low carb + protein + veggies)
* **Hydration** → 3–3.5L water/day

---

### **Workout Plan (6 Days/Week, 1 Rest Day)**

**Day 1–6:**

| Exercise                           | Sets | Reps              | Rest   |
| ---------------------------------- | ---- | ----------------- | ------ |
| Jumping Jacks                      | 3    | 40 sec            | 30 sec |
| High Knees                         | 3    | 40 sec            | 30 sec |
| Push-Ups (knee push-ups if needed) | 3    | 12–15             | 60 sec |
| Squats                             | 3    | 15                | 60 sec |
| Plank                              | 3    | 30–45 sec         | 45 sec |
| Mountain Climbers                  | 3    | 20 per leg        | 30 sec |
| Glute Bridges                      | 3    | 15                | 30 sec |
| Russian Twists (bodyweight)        | 3    | 20 (10 each side) | 30 sec |
| Burpees                            | 3    | 10–12             | 60 sec |

**Day 7:** Complete Rest (light walking or stretching only)
